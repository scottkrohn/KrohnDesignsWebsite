About Krohn Designs

We live and work in beautiful Scottsdale, Arizona. The idea for our shop started when my fiance Jennifer spent months searching the internet and stores for the perfect chalkboard for our home. After giving up the endless search, I decided that we should make our own! We discovered we loved doing it and have been making them non-stop ever since!

We really enjoy making our chalkboards, and it shows in the quality and craftsmanship. We spend a lot of time searching for awesome pieces of wood with cool knots and interesting markings so that each and every chalkboard is one of a kind. You can be sure that when you get your chalkboard it will be unique.

We love making custom projects, so if you've got something in mind and want to see if we can make it, just let us know and we would be happy to help!

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us, or check out our Frequently Asked Questions page.
