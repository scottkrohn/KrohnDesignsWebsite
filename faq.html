How long will my order take to ship?

Generally our production time-frame is 5-10 business days depending on the item. You can check your receipt e-mail from Etsy for your exact ship date. Once we've shipped your order we will send you a tracking number via e-mail. If you haven't placed an order yet, you can check our most accurate production estimate on our Etsy policies page here.

How do you ship your orders?

We ship all of our orders with either UPS, FedEx or USPS. We always provide insurace and tracking on all orders due to the size and weight of our products.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes absolutely! You can visit the "shipping" section on any of our Etsy listings to see how much shipping costs for your country. Or you can contact us directly for a quote.

Do you accept returns?

Returns are handeled on a case by case basis. The buyer is responsible for return shipping unless the return is due to a manufacturing defect in the item. Returns for custom orders are not accepted except in the case of a manufacturing defect.

I ordered the wrong size/color chalkboard, can I exchange it?

We do allow exchanges for another size/color chalkboard, however the buyer is responsible for the difference in cost of the replacement chalkboard, the shipping cost of returning the old chalkboard and shipping the new one.

I accidently ordered non-magnetic instead of a magnetic. What do I do?

This situation is the same as for ordering the wrong size/color. We will be happy to make you a magnetic version of your chalkboard, but you're responsible for the cost different between magnetic/non-magnetic, shipping the old chalkboard back to us and shipping the new chalkboard.

My order arrived with damage from shipping. What do I do?

If you received your order and it was damaged in shipping please let us know immediately. We purchase insurance for all of our items. Once you let us know that your order arrived damaged, we will re-make and re-ship your item and file a claim with the shipper so that no extra costs are necessary for you and for us.

Do your chalkboards work with chalk markers?

Yes! Our chalkboards work great with traditional chalk and with chalkmarkers.

What's the best way to clean my chalkboard?

If you're using chalk markers it will depend on the manufacturer. Some manufacturers simply suggest soap and water while others suggest an ammonia based cleaner. Both methods will are safe to use on your chalkboard. We've found some chalk marker brands are harder than others to clean off but a "Magic Eraser" sponge and water will remove any type!

If you're using traditional chalk you can use a standard chalkboard eraser or for a "like new" clean use a damp wash cloth.
