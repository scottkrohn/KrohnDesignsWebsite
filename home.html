Handmade rustic chalkboards that are great for weddings, gifts, special events and more! All of our chalkboards are made with care using quality materials and tools. We offer both magnetic and non-magnetic chalkboards.

We sand, stain, cut, paint, and distress our frames so that every single frame is unique and one-of-a-kind.

We take great pride in spending a lot of time finding the most beautiful wood with interesting knots, awesome grain patterns and different features so that our chalkboards have character and personality. We use high quality stains to bring out the amazing wood patterns so that your can be sure your chalkboard will be unique.
